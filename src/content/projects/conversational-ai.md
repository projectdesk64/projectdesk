## Overview
Transform your customer support with our Conversational AI. Unlike simple rule-based bots, this system understands context, intent, and sentiment, handling up to 80% of routine queries without human intervention.

## Key Features
- **Natural Language Understanding (NLU)**: accurately deciphers user intent.
- **Seamless Handoff**: intelligently routes complex issues to human agents with full conversation history.
- **Multi-Channel Support**: Deploy on Web, WhatsApp, Telegram, and Slack simultaneously.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: View common queries, drop-off rates, and customer satisfaction scores.

## Technical Specifications
- **Model Training**: Custom fine-tuned transformer models for specific industry domains.
- **Infrastructure**: Dockerized containers orchestrated for auto-scaling during high traffic.
- **Integration**: Webhooks and APIs to connect with existing CRMs like Salesforce or Zendesk.
