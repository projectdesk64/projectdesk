## Overview
Stop manually reading thousands of resumes. Our AI-Powered Resume Screening System automatically parses resumes, matches skills and experience against your job descriptions, and ranks candidates to help you find the top talent faster.

## Key Features
- **Contextual Matching**: Goes beyond keyword matching to understand semantic relevance of skills.
- **Bias Reduction**: Configurable to hide names and photos to promote fair hiring practices.
- **Bulk Processing**: Upload ZIP files of resumes and get a ranked list in minutes.
- **Note Taking & Collaboration**: HR teams can leave notes and share candidate profiles.

## Technical Specifications
- **Parser Engine**: Uses advanced NLP to extract entities like Name, Education, and Experience.
- **Backend**: Robust Django Rest Framework APIs.
- **Database**: PostgreSQL for structured efficient querying of candidate data.
