## Overview
Understand the voice of your customer. This tool aggregates feedback from emails, surveys, and social media, assigning sentiment scores to help you spot trends, identify unhappy customers, and improve service quality.

## Key Features
- **Aspect-Based Sentiment**: Detects sentiment towards specific features (e.g., "Good price" vs "Bad service").
- **Trend Detection**: Alerts management if negative sentiment spikes suddenly.
- **Visual Reports**: Interactive Tableau dashboards for deep diving into data.
- **Keyword Extraction**: Identifies most frequently mentioned pain points.

## Technical Specifications
- **NLP Engine**: Uses VADER and custom trained models for domain-specific nuance.
- **Data Pipeline**: Automated ETL pipelines to fetch and clean data.
- **Visualization**: Embedded Tableau views or custom D3.js charts.
