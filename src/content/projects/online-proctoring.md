## Overview
Ensure the integrity of online exams with this robust Proctoring System. Using computer vision and audio analysis, it monitors students for suspicious behavior such as looking away frequently, unauthorized people in the frame, or potential tab switching.

## Key Features
- **Gaze Tracking**: Estimates where the user is looking to detect off-screen cheating aids.
- **Person Detection**: Alerts if more than one person is detected in the video feed.
- **Browser Locking**: Enforces full-screen mode and prevents copy-pasting.
- **Automated Flagging**: Generates a fast-forward report for instructors with timestamped incidents.

## Technical Specifications
- **Video Streaming**: WebRTC based low-latency streaming.
- **Vision Engine**: OpenCV and Dlib for efficient facial landmark detection.
- **Scalability**: Capable of handling hundreds of concurrent exam sessions.
